03 · Foundations
Shape, elevation & spacing
Medium-round corners — modern iOS, friendly but not bubbly. Our signature surface is the Card: a soft, zero-blur drop in a warm neutral, matched to a 2.5px outline of the very same tone, so every panel gets a gentle hand-drawn lift instead of a heavy glow. On small screens the offset halves. Spacing steps in multiples of 4.

04 · People
Avatars
Families upload real photos. Until then, every person gets a cheerful generated avatar — a soft pop-palette gradient with their initial. The color is assigned per person and stays put, so faces are recognizable across the app.
